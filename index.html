SHONA KELLY

Shona Kelly is a Scottish born writer who was a musician first, then artist, and eventually a writer. Her previous callings are expressed heavily through her writing, and often combined with her artwork. She loves subverting expectations, transgressive themes, the cyberpunk genre, conlang, playing and writing TTRPGs, and pink.
